## 60-Second Tutorial Script

1. Open the Actor and keep the default `RELIANCE` input.
2. Run with financial statements and shareholding off for the cheapest first result.
3. Open the dataset and show price, market cap, P/E, P/B, ROE, ROCE, sector, and source status.
4. Turn on `includeFinancials` for a small symbol list to show quarterly and annual arrays.
5. Export to CSV or Excel, or copy the Apify API endpoint for dashboards.
